1 Introduction

KJ Delivery Services ("we", "our", "us") operates the KJ Delivery mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

By using KJ Delivery, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the application.

2 Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Name, email address, and phone number when you create an account
  • Google account information if you sign in with Google
  • Profile information you choose to provide

Delivery Information

  • Delivery addresses you enter or save
  • Location data when you use the map picker to set a delivery point
  • Delivery instructions and preferences

Order Information

  • Products you browse, add to cart, and purchase
  • Order history, payment method selection, and transaction details
  • Communications with merchants or drivers related to orders

Device & Usage Information

  • Device type, operating system, and app version
  • Usage patterns and interaction data within the app
  • Crash logs and performance data to improve app stability

3 How We Use Your Information

  • Provide services: Process orders, calculate delivery fees, facilitate payments, and enable communication between customers, merchants, and drivers
  • Improve experience: Personalize recommendations, optimize delivery routes, and enhance app performance
  • Communication: Send order updates, delivery notifications, and customer support responses
  • Safety & security: Detect fraud, verify accounts, and protect all users of the platform
  • Legal compliance: Fulfill legal obligations and enforce our terms of service

4 Location Data

KJ Delivery uses location data to:

  • Calculate accurate delivery fees based on distance
  • Allow you to set delivery addresses using the map picker
  • Enable drivers to navigate to delivery locations
  • Show nearby stores and services

Location data is only collected when you actively use location features (such as the map picker). We do not track your location in the background.

5 Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud infrastructure provider with enterprise-grade security. We implement:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Row-level security policies on all database tables
  • Secure authentication with session management
  • Regular security reviews and updates

While we strive to protect your information, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but take all reasonable measures to protect your data.

6 Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google Maps Platform: For map display, location picking, and distance calculation
  • Google Sign-In: For optional social authentication
  • Google Gemini AI: For the AI assistant feature (AI Mate)
  • Supabase: For backend services, authentication, and data storage

Each third-party service operates under its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review their policies.

7 Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data only in these circumstances:

  • With merchants: Order details necessary to fulfill your purchase
  • With drivers: Delivery address and contact information for order delivery
  • Legal requirements: When required by law or to protect rights and safety
  • With your consent: Any other sharing only with your explicit permission

  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete information

  • Withdraw consent for optional data processing
